Start Your Application

Begin your journey at Tranch University by filling out our straightforward online application form. The process is designed to be user-friendly, ensuring you can easily provide the necessary information.

Understand Our Admission Requirements

To qualify for admission, you must pass a standardized test and maintain a minimum GPA of 3.00. This ensures that all our students are academically prepared to succeed in our rigorous programs.

As part of the application process, you are required to upload your Year 11 test results. This helps us assess your academic performance and readiness for university-level studies.

Financial Aid and Scholarships

Tranch University offers generous tuition discounts based on your GPA:

  • 50% Discount: Available for students with a GPA of 3.95 or higher.
  • 60% Discount: Awarded to students who achieve a perfect GPA.

Our financial aid program is designed to reward academic excellence, making quality education more accessible.

Information for International Students

International applicants must demonstrate fluency in English to ensure they can fully participate in our academic programs. This requirement is essential for your success both in and out of the classroom.
